01 — The Problem

Engineers waste hours finding parts they could find in minutes.

Every electronic product starts with a connector problem. An engineer knows exactly what the application needs. But translating that into a spec, filtering 847 results, reading datasheets, and waiting two days for a FAE reply — that costs hours per decision. Multiplied across every connector on every board, it costs programs weeks.

The tools haven't changed in twenty years. Distributor search is keyword search. Manufacturer catalogs are PDFs. Cross-reference tools are lookup tables. None of them understand what the engineer is actually trying to build.

⚠️ What Gets Rebuilt

  • !No test coverage (README templates literally say "TODO")
